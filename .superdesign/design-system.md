# TrueBuilder.ai Design System

## Brand Colors
- **Brand Blue**: #3656CE (primary — logo, accents, CTAs)
- **Sky Blue**: #2CB6FA (secondary accent)
- **Black**: #000000 (dark backgrounds, text)
- **White**: #FFFFFF (light text, backgrounds)
- **Phantom**: #1E1E24 (dark surface)
- **Arsenic**: #42424D (dark muted)
- **Graphite**: #6E7185 (muted text)
- **Steel**: #B0BFC0 (light muted)
- **Smoke**: #D5DDE5 (light border)
- **Cloud**: #E6EFF1 (light surface)

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Manrope (Google Font)
- **Weights**: ExtraLight, Light, Regular, Medium, Semibold, Bold, ExtraBold
- Clean geometric sans-serif

## Logo
- Geometric "TB" monogram — angular interlocking shapes
- Full lockup: emblem + "TrueBuilder" (camelCase) + ".ai"
- On dark backgrounds: use white logo variant (Logo-4) or blue emblem + white text (Logo-2)
- SVG available at: trubuilder_logo/Logo files/SVG/

## Design Direction
- Dark, futuristic, AI-themed
- The brand presentation uses dark backgrounds (~#1a1a1f) with blue emblem as watermark
- Headline: "The Future Is Coming"
- Subtext: "Coming Soon"
- Email capture form for early access signups
- Single full-viewport landing page
- Geometric pattern elements as supporting graphics (circles, dot grids, concentric lines per brand kit)
