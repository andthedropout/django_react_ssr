# TrueBuilder.ai Brand Kit

## Logo

- **Icon/Emblem**: Geometric "TB" monogram — angular, interlocking shapes with a forward-leaning diagonal cut. Structural, feels like building blocks / construction.
- **Full lockup**: Emblem + "TrueBuilder" (one word, camelCase) + ".ai" in smaller weight

### Logo Variants
| # | Description | Use Case |
|---|------------|----------|
| Logo-1 | Blue emblem + black text | Light backgrounds |
| Logo-2 | Blue emblem + white text | Dark backgrounds |
| Logo-3 | All black | Monochrome light |
| Logo-4 | All white | Monochrome dark |
| Logo-5 | White on brand blue bg | Brand-colored surfaces |

## Design Notes

- Brand presentation uses **dark backgrounds** (~`#1a1a1f`) with the blue emblem as a subtle watermark — that's the brand's natural dark-mode aesthetic
- Brand blue `#3656CE` is the dominant accent; sky blue `#2CB6FA` is the secondary pop
- Logo needs generous clearspace — don't crowd it
- Social kits use geometric pattern elements (circles, dot grids, concentric lines) as supporting graphics
- The brand guidelines show a newsletter email capture pattern and "The Next Generation" as sample headline copy
